#----------------------------------------
# MountJump
#----------------------------------------
Used to add the jump portion of a Spiritspring.

#-------------------------
# Unique Properties
#-------------------------
UnkT00:                 Multiplier of Max Jump Height
                        - Values should be between 1 and 100
                        - Values below 1 break the jump.
                        - Values above 100 do not increase height.
UnkT04:                 ?
